About Dr. Z

The mind behind tomorrow’s meals.

Dr. Z was a visionary engineer, culinary theorist, and futurist whose work reshaped the modern household. His research focused on eliminating inefficiency in daily life—beginning with food.

Through relentless experimentation, Dr. Z concluded that human cooking introduced unnecessary variability. Meals, he believed, should be precise, repeatable, and optimized.

“Why cook,” Dr. Z famously asked, “when perfection can be automated?”
