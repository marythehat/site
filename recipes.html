    • 
  
  • 3 cups (720ml) heavy cream or heavy whipping cream*
    • 
  
  • 1/2 teaspoon espresso powder (optional but recommended)*
    • 
  
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 
  
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract*
    • 




Instructions




Remove about 1/2 cup of warm heavy cream and, in a slow and steady stream,
whisk into the egg yolks. Keep the yolks moving so they do not scramble.





Slowly pour the egg mixture back into the warm cream. Stir carefully.
Place ramekins into a large baking pan.





Divide custard evenly. Add hot water to the pan.
Bake until edges are set and centers remain slightly jiggly.
