Kitchen Inventions

Innovation, automated.

C.A.L. TABLETOP

The Culinary Assisted Learning (C.A.L.) Tabletop is an all-in-one cooking unit capable of preparing complete meals from raw ingredients. More than an appliance, it actively teaches users how to cook through clear, audible, step-by-step instructions. A simplified mode ensures accessibility for younger or inexperienced users.

SMART TABLE

Designed to adapt to any environment, the Smart Table automatically adjusts its dimensions to fit available space. Integrated heat-bearing “Pot Zones” eliminate the need for hot pads or placemats, allowing cookware to be placed directly on the surface without damage.

HANDS

Created to address multitasking inefficiencies, Hands are a pair of autonomous, levitating assistants capable of handling basic kitchen tasks. Stirring, holding, passing utensils—Hands take over simple actions so users can focus on higher-level decisions.
